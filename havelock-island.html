HAVELOCK ISLAND

The picturesque Havelock Island in the Bay of Bengal attracts tourists from all over the world. Ideal for honeymooners, photography enthusiasts and water sports buffs alike, this island has a more serious official name Swaraj Dweep a tribute to our freedom fighters. Beaches, forests, water sports zones and surrounding islands make up the various places to visit in Havelock. You can enjoy all the tourist hotspots as long as you are careful about Havelock weather.

The island also gives you the opportunity to appreciate nature, wildlife and marine life through a tour of the places to visit in Havelock. Some places like Radhanagar beach and Kalapathar beach are more admired due to their popularity among families and honeymooners.There are loads of things to do on Havelock Island and touring places to visit in Havelock is just one of them. Laze on the beaches and enjoy a cool swim. Indulge in water sports of all types jet skiing, kayaking, banana boat rides and surfing. Explore vibrant marine life with scuba diving and snorkelling. Another thrilling activity is sea walking.

Many of the places to visit in Havelock offer these sports. You can bike or trek to various locations. For an exhilarating experience, go for the 9-seater helicopter ride and get an aerial view of Ross Island, Cellular Jail, North Bay Island and more. Do not forget a ride on the glass-bottomed boats. Put Cellular Jail in the itinerary for places to visit in Havelock to pay homage to the freedom fighters.If your travel seems incomplete without getting into some fun activities then you will love to get indulged in indulging in beach activities and exploring the tourist hotspots.
