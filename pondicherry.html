PONDICHERRY

Pondicherry is the capital and most populous city of the Union Territory of Puducherry in India. The city is in the Puducherry district on the southeast coast of India and is surrounded by the Bay of Bengal to the east and the state of Tamil Nadu, with which it shares most of its culture, heritage, and language. Pondicherry experiences a tropical wet and dry climate. Summers experience temperatures up to 41 °C.

Pondicherry weather is extremely humid during the monsoons from June to September. The best time to visit Pondicherry is during the winter (October to March). The temperature shuttles between 15°C to 30°C making it an ideal time to explore places to visit in Pondicherry. One of the most popular places to visit in Pondicherry, this beach is equally loved by the local people as well as the tourists. The blue waters, golden sand and palm trees will steal your heart.

The beach shacks are a popular joint for the young crowd although consumption of alcohol is not allowed on this beach. Swimming is not advisable on Paradise Beach- Pondicherry but you can try your hands at fishing.
